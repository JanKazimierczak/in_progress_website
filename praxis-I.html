Overview

Problem framing

The project addresses a practical rider problem: carrying a backpack comfortably while using a shared bicycle. The design direction is strongest when framed around ergonomics, stability, manufacturability, and mounting constraints.

What to show
  • How the concept attaches to the bike system.
  • Why the geometry reduces rider discomfort.
  • What tradeoffs were made around weight and stability.
  • What testing or evaluation would validate the design.
