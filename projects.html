Content direction

What makes a project page look stronger

  • Lead with the problem, not the course name.
  • Show constraints, decisions, and results.
  • Keep summaries short enough to scan quickly.
  • Add links only when the supporting page is ready.
What changed here

The original page had commented templates, placeholder cards, and “loading” language. This version accepts that the site is still developing, but frames the work like a real engineering portfolio instead of a partially filled starter layout.
